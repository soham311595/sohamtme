Quantum Computing Stocks Surge: A Look at IonQ, Rigetti, and D-Wave

November 18, 2024

Key Earnings Highlights

November 2024 saw significant movements in the quantum computing sector, particularly for companies like **IonQ**, **Rigetti Computing**, and **D-Wave Quantum**. While all three companies experienced substantial stock price increases during the month, their futures remain tied to the speculative nature of the emerging quantum computing market.

IonQ's Surge

**IonQ (Ticker: IONQ)** saw a remarkable **140% increase** in stock price during November 2024. This growth was fueled by continued advancements in their quantum computing technology, as well as growing investor interest in the potential of quantum computing. However, the stock has shown volatility, with a notable **39% decline in January 2025** following comments from Nvidia's CEO, who stated that useful quantum computing could still be decades away.

Rigetti's Growth

Similarly, **Rigetti Computing (Ticker: RGTI)** experienced an impressive **46% increase** in its stock price in November. The company's focus on building scalable quantum computing systems attracted increased attention. Like IonQ, Rigetti's stock experienced a **47% drop** in January 2025, as concerns about the practical applications of quantum computing dampened investor sentiment.

D-Wave Quantum's Success

**D-Wave Quantum (Ticker: QBTS)** was another standout, with a **125% increase** in stock value. D-Wave has made significant strides in quantum annealing, which contributed to the surge in its stock price. However, similar to its competitors, D-Wave also saw a **42% drop** in January 2025, in line with the broader market trend.
