Personal Portfolio Update – June 2024

Portfolio Breakdown and Performance

Overview

For June 2024, my portfolio is designed to tap into niche opportunities that often go unnoticed by mainstream investors. These companies are positioned in specific industries that show tremendous growth potential but might be underappreciated by the broader market. The following 12 stocks reflect a strategy focused on emerging technologies, specialized sectors, and next-generation solutions.

Portfolio Breakdown

1. MercadoLibre (MELI) – 12.3%

Growth So Far (2024): +57.6%

MercadoLibre is the dominant e-commerce platform in Latin America, benefiting from the region’s growing digital economy. The company is also expanding into fintech through MercadoPago, making it a key player in the intersection of e-commerce and digital finance.

2. Cloudflare (NET) – 11.7%

Growth So Far (2024): +72.3%

Cloudflare is carving out a niche in the cybersecurity and content delivery network space. With an increasing demand for internet security and faster delivery, Cloudflare is well-positioned to benefit from these trends as businesses continue to migrate to the cloud.

3. Unity Software (U) – 8.5%

Growth So Far (2024): +61.9%

Unity has established itself as the go-to engine for game development, but its reach is expanding into industries like automotive, architecture, and healthcare with 3D visualization. The growing use of real-time 3D applications will help Unity sustain strong growth.

4. Upstart (UPST) – 9.4%

Growth So Far (2024): +48.2%

Upstart is revolutionizing the credit scoring system by using AI to assess risk more accurately than traditional models. The potential disruption in lending practices makes Upstart an interesting player as it expands its offerings in personal and auto loans.

5. Elastic (ESTC) – 7.6%

Growth So Far (2024): +54.7%

Elastic is a leader in enterprise search and big data analytics, helping companies pull actionable insights from vast datasets. The shift toward data-driven decision-making, particularly in AI and machine learning, positions Elastic as a significant player in this space.

6. Illumina (ILMN) – 6.8%

Growth So Far (2024): +40.5%

Illumina is a leader in genomics and gene sequencing technologies. With the increasing use of personalized medicine, Illumina’s technologies are critical in driving advancements in healthcare, offering great long-term growth potential.

7. Twilio (TWLO) – 6.3%

Growth So Far (2024): +37.8%

Twilio is well-positioned to capitalize on the demand for cloud communication services. Its broad API offerings that power messaging, voice, and video services are integral to many digital businesses. Its expanding product suite should fuel future growth.

8. Zscaler (ZS) – 5.2%

Growth So Far (2024): +52.1%

Zscaler has a niche in cloud-based security, offering companies a platform to secure their data and applications in the cloud. As enterprises increasingly move their operations to the cloud, demand for Zscaler’s services will continue to rise.

9. Roku (ROKU) – 4.9%

Growth So Far (2024): +28.6%

Roku is a leader in the streaming device market and continues to see growth through its ad-supported services. As the media consumption landscape continues to evolve, Roku stands out by offering an open platform that provides consumers access to a wide range of content.

10. Coupang (CPNG) – 5.7%

Growth So Far (2024): +62.4%

Known as the "Amazon of South Korea," Coupang’s dominance in e-commerce, coupled with its logistics and delivery systems, positions it as a unique growth opportunity in Asia's rapidly expanding digital economy.

11. Doximity (DOCS) – 4.3%

Growth So Far (2024): +79.2%

Doximity is the leading social network for U.S. healthcare professionals, providing them with tools for networking, collaboration, and career development. With healthcare moving towards telemedicine and digital solutions, Doximity’s growth trajectory looks solid.

12. Plug Power (PLUG) – 5.6%

Growth So Far (2024): +64.3%

Plug Power is one of the leaders in the hydrogen fuel cell industry, focusing on clean energy solutions. As the global push for decarbonization accelerates, Plug Power stands to benefit significantly from increased demand for alternative energy solutions.

Performance Summary

Overall Growth of Portfolio (2024): +56.1%

Best Performer: Doximity (+79.2%)

Worst Performer: Roku (+28.6%)

Conclusion

This portfolio focuses on niche players within growing sectors such as AI, cybersecurity, healthcare, and clean energy. As of June 2024, the portfolio has seen substantial growth, especially with companies like Doximity and Upstart leading the way. These niche players not only offer significant upside potential but also have the flexibility to scale with changing market demands. Their unique offerings within their respective industries make them exciting additions to my long-term investment strategy.
