Klarna's AI Assistant Revolutionizes Customer Service

February 27, 2024

In a significant advancement for the fintech industry, Klarna, the global payments network and shopping assistant, announced the launch of its AI-powered assistant on January 27, 2024. This innovative tool, developed in collaboration with OpenAI, has transformed Klarna's customer service operations, handling two-thirds of customer service chats within its first month of deployment.

The AI assistant has conducted over 2.3 million conversations, effectively performing the work equivalent to 700 full-time agents. Impressively, it has achieved customer satisfaction scores on par with human agents and has reduced repeat inquiries by 25%, leading to a more efficient and responsive customer service experience. Additionally, the average time to resolve customer issues has decreased from 11 minutes to under 2 minutes, significantly enhancing operational efficiency.

Available 24/7 across 23 markets and fluent in more than 35 languages, the AI assistant has also improved communication with local immigrant and expat communities, providing support in their native languages. This accessibility has broadened Klarna's reach and improved customer engagement globally.

Financially, Klarna anticipates that the AI assistant will contribute an additional $40 million in profit by the end of 2024, underscoring the substantial impact of AI integration on the company's bottom line.

This development highlights the growing role of artificial intelligence in transforming customer service within the financial sector, setting a new standard for efficiency and customer satisfaction.
